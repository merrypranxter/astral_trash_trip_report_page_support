# tr-0002 — ASCII Idea Seeds
> The Unstuffing (shower scrub ritual — recurring pattern report). CONCEPTUAL GUIDES for
> Google AI Studio — density maps, charsets, chaos/order logic. Not final art.
> Water = cleansing, vertical, one-way. No nautical motifs.

---

## Seed 01 — THE UNSTUFFING
**Logic:** crust vs. light, resolved by strokes.
A central figure built of luminous `█▓` (the form) coated in a noise-crust of `;:.,``'
(the stuff, thin and drab). Vertical water columns `| | |` rain from the top edge.
Two bright stroke-zones (the gloved hands, rendered as moving `@@` patches) sweep the
figure in rhythmic arcs; everywhere they pass, the crust chars detach and drift downward
as `,,,` until they exit the bottom edge. The figure glows one brightness-step higher
behind each stroke. The crust slowly re-accumulates. The loop IS the ritual.
**Density:** 70% (crust zones) strobing down to 30% (freshly scrubbed).
**Charset:** form `█▓▒`, crust `;:.,`, water `|`, hands `@@`, shed matter `,`.
**Motion:** rain at 1x; strokes at 2–3 Hz; re-accumulation barely visible.

## Seed 02 — BACKGROUND PROCESS ("I want out")
**Logic:** the text is always there; you only see it in the gaps.
Foreground: dense, pleasant bathroom texture — tile grid `┼┼┼`, sparkle `*`, steam `~~`,
all in constant gentle motion. Behind it: `I WANT OUT` repeated in a massive grid,
characters as tall as the tile rows, scrolling almost imperceptibly. Every ~40 seconds
the foreground drops to scattered dots `· · ·` for two seconds — and the loop is suddenly
all there is. Then the tiles come back. The viewer's job changes after the first reveal:
they start watching the gaps.
**Density:** foreground 85% → 5% during reveals.
**Charset:** foreground `┼+*~`, loop text = plain block letters (no decoration — system energy).
**Motion:** foreground idle drift; loop scrolls at 1 char/2s; reveals are abrupt.

## Seed 03 — DRAIN VORTEX (one-way exit)
**Logic:** the bottom row eats everything.
Upper two-thirds: the shower scene — falling water `|`, shed flakes `,`. Bottom third:
a vortex rendered as concentric spiral arms `@ % # + : .` tightening toward a center
drain `(█)`. Flakes that cross the vortex line get captured: their characters spiral,
accelerate, shrink down the luminance ramp, and vanish into `(█)`. Nothing crosses
back upward. Every few seconds a flake attempts an eddy-escape — the spiral visibly
corrects it back in. Order without cruelty.
**Density:** 40% upper / 90% vortex zone.
**Charset:** spiral `@%#+:.` by radius, drain `(█)`, flakes `,`.
**Motion:** spiral 0.2 rev/s; capture events per-flake; one-way rule absolute.

## Seed 04 — THE INQUIRY (peppermint mind)
**Logic:** the question renders colder as it gets clearer.
A thought-cloud of jumbled warm characters `&%#@§?` (the trip-noise) with one sentence
trying to form inside it: `what do i want off of me`. Each loop, more of the cloud's
characters get replaced by cold-clean `-` and `=` (peppermint clarifying), and the
sentence resolves one word sharper. When the sentence completes, the cloud flashes its
answer by EXCLUSION: the word `skin` appears and is visibly struck through (`s̶k̶i̶n̶` →
replaced by `~~~~`), then the word `stuff` appears and STAYS, glowing `@`-bright.
The cloud goes quiet. The scrubbing can begin.
**Density:** 60% → 20% as clarity increases.
**Charset:** noise `&%#@§?`, clarity `-=`, the answer words in plain text, kept-answer glow `@`.
**Motion:** slow resolve (~30s), one flash beat for the rejection, then stillness.

## Cross-seed constants
- The ritual is devotional maintenance, not crisis — no panic charsets, no alarm energy.
- Water moves down. Stuff moves down. Light stays. Those are the only traffic rules.
- The background-process seed (02) is the signature piece: text-behind-the-world is this report's whole phenomenology.
- No vessels, no sea, no anchors. The shower is a chamber, not an ocean.
